Meet Jase

When you contact J.R Automotive, you are not dealing with a call centre or being passed from person to person. You are speaking with someone who understands the job, listens to what is going on, and helps point you in the right direction.

The aim is simple: explain things clearly, do quality work, and stand by what we do.

J.R Automotive is not trying to be the cheapest workshop in town. The focus is on doing the job properly, using good parts, giving honest advice, and helping customers make informed decisions about their vehicles.

Whether your car is due for a service, has failed a WOF, has a warning light on, or just does not feel right, Jason, Amanda, and the team are here to make the process easier.
